Migo Taskcare

Project information

  • Category: Mobile and Backend Application
  • Project Type: Company Project
  • Company: Migo Entertainment Inc.
  • Project URL: Migo Taskcare

A Ticketing System that allows full customizability to support custom requirements and reduces operational costs by $1,000 USD per month. It is used by the company as the main Operational Ticketing Platform that focuses on tracking different types of issues and tasks for various teams, including Finance, RTM, Marketing, Production, and more. This platform's key functions include Account and access management, Ticket management, Store management, and Dashboards for SLAs and performance reporting.

Built with Android/Kotlin for the app and Angular, NodeJS, ExpressJS, and Firestore, BigQuery and Data Studio for full-stack web development.
