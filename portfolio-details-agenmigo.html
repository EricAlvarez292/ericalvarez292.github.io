AgenMigo

Project information

  • Category: Mobile and Backend Application
  • Project Type: Company Project
  • Company: Migo Entertainment Inc.

Designed, developed, and maintained an in-house Agency Tool, an incentive program platform that is driven by Sales Team to facilitates incentive program management, agent account/organizational management and sales dashboard. Boosting company sales by 25% and providing extra income to 3000+ external agents. Built with Android/Kotlin for App and (Angular, NodeJS/Express and PostgreSQL) for full stack Web.
