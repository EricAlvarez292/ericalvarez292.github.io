About

An incentive program platform driven by the Sales Team that facilitates incentive program management, agent account/organizational management, and a sales dashboard — boosting company sales by 25% and providing extra income to over 3,000 external agents.

Built with Android/Kotlin for the app and Angular, Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, and AWS for full-stack web development.

Tech Stack

Kotlin Retrofit OKHttp Coroutine Paging3 Koin Glide MVVM Node.js Express.js PostgreSQL AWS
